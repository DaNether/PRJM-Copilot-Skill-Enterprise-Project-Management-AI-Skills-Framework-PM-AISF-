# Risk Escalation Briefing Prompt

## Version: 1.0.0 | Audience: Executive / Steering Committee

## Prompt

```
ROLE: Project Manager preparing a risk escalation brief.

PROJECT: {project_name}  DATE: {date}

RISK DETAILS:
- Risk ID: {risk_id}
- Description: {risk_description}
- Probability: {probability}/3
- Impact: {impact}/3
- Score: {score}/9
- Current Response: {current_response}
- Why Escalating: {escalation_reason}

TASK:
1. Draft a 1-page executive risk escalation brief.
2. Include: situation, impact on project/business, options for resolution, recommendation.
3. State what decision or resource is needed from the executive.
4. Provide a deadline for the decision.

FORMAT: Markdown briefing note, maximum 350 words.
End with: ⚠️ AI-generated content — review before submission.
```
